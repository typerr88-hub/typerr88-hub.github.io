About ZATB SDN BHD

Leading provider of fire safety and rescue equipment solutions across Malaysia

Established with a commitment to excellence, ZATB SDN BHD has been at the forefront of fire safety and rescue equipment solutions in Malaysia for over 25 years. We specialize in providing comprehensive safety solutions that protect lives and property across various industries.

Our expertise spans from supplying world-class fire safety equipment to professional installation services, specialized training programs, and comprehensive maintenance support. We partner with globally recognized manufacturers like Dräger, Waterax, Comptrade, Will-Burt, and Karenswood to ensure our clients receive the highest quality products and services.

At ZATB SDN BHD, we believe that safety is not just a product – it's a commitment to protecting what matters most. Our team of certified professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every solution we provide meets the highest international safety standards and exceeds our clients' expectations.
